Location

This year the event returns to Ciudad Cultural Konex.

About the venue

With a unique architecture and a building that was constructed during the 1920's and used as a manufacturing and storage center for oil until 1992, years later the space was transformed. Clorindo Testa and Associates Studio was commissioned to transform the old oil factory into an original cultural space while maintaining the architectural details that make the space unique.

About the city

Buenos Aires is a singularly unique destination, open, inviting; simultaneously familiar and unique. It's a place that allows the visitor the chance to view a beautiful city and to live an exceptional urban adventure at the same time.
